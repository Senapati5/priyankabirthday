HAPPY BIRTHDAY 🎂

No matter where life has taken us,

I hope today reminds you how loved you are.

Our paths may have parted,

but I still believe you deserve

all the beautiful things life has to offer.

Even if we no longer share our lives,

I’ll never forget how much your friendship meant.

I still remember the laughter

and the late-night talks.

You were important to me,

even if we aren’t part of each other’s stories anymore.

We outgrew each other,

and that's part of growing up.

Not every friendship is meant to last forever,

but that doesn’t mean it wasn’t real.

You were my person once,

and a part of me will always miss that.

We may not talk anymore like before,

but I still smile at the memories.

I didn't know if this was special for you,

But I did my best to make the day perfect for you ❤️
